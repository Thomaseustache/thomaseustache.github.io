About the Project

Nomadspot is a travel planning app that helps users discover and organize their trips. As the sole creator of the project, I handled every aspect from ideation to execution, including designing the user experience, developing multiple versions of the app, conducting user testing, and refining the product. The entire project reflects my dedication to creating a seamless and intuitive tool for group travel planning.
