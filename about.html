Thomas Eustache

About

Holder of a bachelor's degree in Multimedia Project Management from HETIC, I have over 10 years of experience in graphic design and web development, crafting engaging digital experiences for clients ranging from startups to established businesses.

I began my career at TBWA (Being / Zakka), where I developed creative solutions for leading brands, followed by impactful projects at Mutum, a collaborative economy startup. I then joined Wonderstudios, focusing on crafting visually compelling and interactive digital experiences, and later honed my skills in e-commerce at Ebrand Commerce.

As a HETIC instructor, I shared my expertise with aspiring designers and developers, mentoring students in mastering design principles and creative development.

Currently, I work as a freelance designer and developer, collaborating with French and international clients. I specialize in building tailored websites, sleek admin dashboards, and unique interactive projects that align with brand strategies.

From crafting impactful branding visuals to developing engaging digital tools, my passion lies in creating solutions that combine aesthetics and functionality, helping businesses stand out in a competitive digital landscape.
